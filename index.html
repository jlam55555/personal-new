About

I'm currently a senior in high school, entering the Cooper Union as a freshman next year. While I will be studying electrical engineering, my passion is creating applications that will ease the life of workers, students, and other programmers. I hope that a college education in EE will allow me to get a fundamental idea of how computers work, while learning the theoretical side of computer science on my own.

I enjoy spending free time bowling, Rubik's cubing, doing origami, listening to Studio Ghibli music, answering questions on Stack Overflow, trying to solve problems on Project Euler or Code Wars, and learning new languages. I also maintain a literary and programming blog.
